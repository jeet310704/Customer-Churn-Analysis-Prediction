BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS
-------------------------
1. Offer retention discounts to Month-to-Month customers —
   this is the highest-impact segment (65% churn rate).

2. Incentivise Electronic Check customers to switch to auto-pay —
   this payment method has a 66% churn rate.

3. Improve support experience for high-call-volume customers —
   those with 4+ calls are disproportionately likely to churn.

4. Focus onboarding resources on the first 12 months —
   new customers on Month-to-Month contracts are the highest flight risk.

5. Use model predictions to prioritise the retention team's outreach
   by targeting customers with the highest predicted churn probability.

6. Proactively engage customers paying more than $80/month —
   this group has a 62.7% churn rate and represents high revenue at risk.
